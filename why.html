The Founder

I'm Deâvion McIntosh, an alumna from Spelman College with a Bachelor's of Arts in psychology from Bronx, New York.

In 2022 I incorporated my love for travelling with application development to illustrate the beauties of the world to others.

Dream weekend began as a fun project where I would plan a trip with my cohort members for a fun weekend and curate amazing memories, but also show them Caribbean culture. It took off from there and since then I kept it going.

All I'm going to say is Dream Weekend is a movie. A true movement that you don't want to miss, and what happens at Dream Weekend stays at Dream Weekend.

What is Dream Weekend?

Dream Weekend 2023 is a 5-6 day trip for LCJ members to spend time with other cohort members and create exciting memories.

Why Come to Dream Weekend?

Being part of Year Up was an experience that only those who has done it will be able to explain.

After this process, you should spend time out the country living lavishly and having fun before we enter the workforce.

Once this trip is over, we will all leave as family and this trip will definitely bring us all closer.

Where Will We Stay?

As a cohort, we will decide on one location and afterwards we will be staying at a 5 star all inclusive resort that comes with all you can eat and drink.
